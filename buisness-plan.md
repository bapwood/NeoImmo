## Market Analysis

## INDUSTRY OVERVIEW

Over the past five years, the cryptocurrency investment market has grown significantly, driven by increasing adoption from both retail and institutional investors, the rise of centralized exchanges, decentralized finance (DeFi), and more recently crypto-related financial products such as ETFs. Despite periods of volatility, the overall trend shows strong long-term growth and a gradual normalization of crypto as an asset class within diversified portfolios. At the same time, competition has intensified with major centralized platforms dominating user acquisition, while DeFi protocols and emerging blockchain-based investment solutions continue to innovate around accessibility, yield generation, and asset tokenization. Neoimmo positions itself at the intersection of these trends by combining real-world assets (real estate) with blockchain-based investment mechanisms, in a space that remains relatively underdeveloped compared to pure crypto trading or traditional real estate investing.

## Marketing & Sales Strategy

## Brand Positioning

Neoimmo positions itself as an accessible and transparent real estate investment platform that enables anyone to invest in property through fractional ownership. The brand emphasizes simplicity, trust, and financial empowerment, while removing technical complexity related to blockchain and making the experience understandable for non-crypto users.

## Lead Generation Channels

Our user acquisition strategy focuses on building trust and educating users through:

- Content marketing (educational posts explaining real estate investing and accessibility)
- Social media (Instagram, LinkedIn) to build awareness and community
- Whitelist and early-access campaigns to capture early adopters
- Community building through newsletters and digital engagement
- Strategic partnerships (to complete: real estate partners, financial communities, etc.)

## Sales Funnel Strategy

We guide users through a simple and transparent journey:

- Awareness: users discover Neoimmo through content and social channels
- Education: users understand the concept of fractional real estate and platform benefits
- Conversion: users join the platform and purchase tokens representing property shares
- Retention: users receive monthly rental income and manage their portfolio on the platform
- Expansion: users reinvest, diversify their portfolio, and increase holdings

## Year 1 Financial Projections

Neoimmo’s revenue model is driven by transaction fees on tokenized property sales and recurring management fees from rental income. As the platform acquires and monetizes multiple real estate assets, revenue scales with total assets under management and user adoption, while maintaining a lean operational structure in the early stage.
